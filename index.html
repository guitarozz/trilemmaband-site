About

TriLemma is a musical experience. We are a dynamic group of musicians who blend classic and modern tunes to create an unforgettable live show. Our performances include Classic Rock, Blues, 80s, 90s, Soul, and original tunes.

We are based in Saint Joseph, Missouri, and perform regularly at local venues and events. Whether you're looking for a night of dancing or just want to enjoy great music, TriLemma has something for everyone.
