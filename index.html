About

TriLemma is:

  • Brandon O'Brien: Piano, Keys, Synths and Vocals
  • Jason Osborn: Guitar and Vocals
  • Adam Park: Drums and Vocals

TriLemma started as the Jason & Brandon Duo in 2021 and evolved into a hard hitting trio with the addition of drummer Adam Park. Today, the band blends original music with a wide-ranging set of covers—from the soulful grit of The Red Clay Strays to the high-energy hits of Nelly—all delivered with a focus on connection, not just performance. We don’t play at the crowd—we play with them. Every show is built to pull people in, whether that means a packed dance floor or a room locked into the moment. TriLemma is currently writing new original music, with plans to enter the studio in 2026.

We are based in Saint Joseph, Missouri, and perform regularly at local venues and events. Whether you're looking for a night of dancing or just want to enjoy great music, TriLemma has something for everyone.
